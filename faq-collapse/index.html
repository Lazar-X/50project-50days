Frequently Asked Questions

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Can I cancel my order once it has been placed?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it is shipped. Once it is shipped, we cannot cancel the order.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times depend on your location and the shipping method you choose at checkout. Generally, it takes 3-5 business days for domestic orders and 7-14 business days for international orders.

What is your return policy?

We have a 30-day return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a refund or exchange within 30 days of receiving it.
